100 days of SVG is a 100 day project in the spirit of perseverence and iteration. For 100 days I will create one illustration using colour, scalable vector graphics, and typography.

100 days of fonts by Doo-Hee Kim was my first exposure to the 100 days project, and through it I've found projects and tools like Google Type Pairings and Coloors that got me stoked on making.

My interests ping-pong between illustration, web development, typography, and 100 other things, and I like finding ways to rope them together. Welcome to Exhibit A!
