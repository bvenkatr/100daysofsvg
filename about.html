What is this?

100 days of SVG is a 100 day project in the spirit of perseverence and iteration. For 100 days I will create one illustration using colour, scalable vector graphics (SVG), and typography.

Who else?

100 days of fonts by Doo-Hee Kim was my first exposure to the 100 days project, and through it I’ve found exciting projects like Print a Day and Aesop Google Type (which I contributed to if you like yourself a little type-setting).

Check out my favourites:

  • 100 Days of Fonts
  • 100/100/100


My interests ping-pong between illustration, web development, typography, and 100 other things, and I like finding ways to rope them together. Welcome to Exhibit A!
