What is this?

100 days of SVG is a 100 day project in the spirit of perseverance and iteration. For 100 days I will create one illustration using colour, scalable vector graphics (SVG), and typography. Each tile, one per day, will be coded live on the site using as little pre-prepared images and as much html and css as possible.

Who else?

100 days of fonts by Doo-Hee Kim was my first exposure to the 100 days project, and through it I’ve found neat projects like Colorrrs and Aesop Google Type (which I contributed to if you like yourself a little type-setting).
